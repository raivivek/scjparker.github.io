Islet eQTL variants A tool for exploring variants in islet expression quantitative trait loci. The data is the result of a collaboration between our lab, the Department of Biostatistics and Center for Statistical Genetics at the University of Michigan, the National Human Genome Research Institute, the Jackson Laboratory for Genomic Medicine, the Department of Genetics at the University of North Carolina, the European Bioinformatics Institute, the Department of Preventive Medicine at the University of Southern California, and the Department of Physiology and Biophysics at the University of Southern California.
